Our Quality & Resilience Framework

Our systems and processes will aspire to embed best practice international principals and guidelines, seek certification to ensure we have the discipline to do the right thing and a mechanism for continuous improvement. We partner with these key members in the field who act as our Seals of Trust to reassure our stakeholders.

Certification

  • Global GAP Certification - Good Agriculture Practice
  • GRASP GAP Certification - GRASP is committed to Workers' Health, Safety, and Welfare
  • BSI Kite Mark - Quality across the chain of custody
  • B-CORP - for the social and environmental impact

Key Partnerships

  • BSI [British Standards Institution] supporting with best-practice standards to ensure we remain future-ready
  • TUV SUD - Testing services to monitor and ensure minimum residue levels.

Membership

  • Agricultural and Processed Food Products Export Development Authority (APEDA) is an Indian Apex-Export Trade Promotion Active government body
  • Food Safety and Standards Authority of India (FSSAI) is a statutory body established under the Ministry of Health & Family Welfare, Government of India.
